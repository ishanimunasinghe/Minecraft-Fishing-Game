Welcome to Minecraft Fishing! Here are the instructions:

1. Click on one of the above characters based on which type of fish you want to catch. For example, Steve catches a lot of raw cod while the villager catches mostly enchanted books.

2. Once you start fishing, there are two ways you can catch fish: either by pushing on the 'fish for me' buttons on the left-hand side of your screen or by tapping on the fish as they appear on the screen.

3. Watch the tracker to see how many fish you catch. Try to get the most number of fish before the time runs out!

4. Click on the button below to start fishing!
